Request Account Deletion

Updated July 01, 2024

ChatCupid is committed to user privacy and protecting your data. If you wish to delete your personal data associated with our app, here are the steps you need to follow:

1. Send us an email:

Send an email to support@chatcupid.app from the email address associated with your ChatCupid account.

2. Subject Line:

Please include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line of the email.

3. Data that will be deleted:

Upon your request, we will delete your account and all associated data, including personal information, profiles, messages, and interaction history from our system.

4. Data that will be retained:

We may retain some anonymized data for analytical purposes, but this data will not be associated with your identity. Certain data may be kept as required by law for a limited period.
